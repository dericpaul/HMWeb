Welcome to JMMCRI

Jubilee Mission Medical College Hospital and Research Institute is one of the pioneering institutions in Health Care and Education in South India. Following the motto, 'Service with Love', Jubilee Mission concentrates on optimum care for the patients, irrespective of caste and creed. Lovingly called by the people of Thrissur, "Poor Man's Hospital", JMMCRI has an existential drive to uphold 'preferential option' for the poor and provide special considerations and concessions. Taking the mandate from Jesus Christ the Healer, Jubilee Mission serves the holistic welfare of patients and the society at large, responding to the Need of Hour.

Imbibing the Catholic momentum in teaching, JMMCRI is equipped to impart high quality education in the Health Care. Jubilee Mission Medical College and Related Health Education endeavours excel in their mission without losing the true Christian Spirit. JMMCRI finds its fulfilment in making this place a Happy Home to re-launch their lives to better future with renewed vigour.
